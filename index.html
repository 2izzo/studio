Rizzo Studio

Music theory you can hear.

Five packs. Each pack is a short run of interactive lessons — click, listen, play along — that build one idea until you can hear it without thinking. Pick a pack to start or resume.

20 lessons across 5 packs.
Progress lives in your browser's localStorage. Each pack stores its own key (rizzo-studio-<pack>). Use the "reset module progress" link inside a pack to clear just that pack, or clear all site data to reset everything.
