The Experiment

Try this: hit play on Pop. Let it loop twice so your ear locks in. Then switch to Doo-Wop without stopping. Notice how the vi chord appearing in a different slot completely shifts the emotional arc. Nothing else changed — same key, same tempo, same Rhodes. That's the power of numerals.
