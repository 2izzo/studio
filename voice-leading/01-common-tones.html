What you're hearing

The green notes stay — same pitch, both chords. Your ear hears them as the anchor. The warm notes are the ones that have to actually move, and the number of semitones they travel is the "cost" of the move. Low cost = smooth. High cost = the chord change feels like a jump cut. Same four notes in different voicings can make a 5-semitone move or a 1-semitone move. That's the whole game.
